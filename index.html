My Top 8 Favorite Anime

This is a small portfolio of my favorite anime from over the years! Among various captivating anime series, a few stand out as personal favorites, each offering a blend of thought-provoking narratives and unforgettable characters. 'Paradise Kiss' shares a tale of self-discovery and love set against the backdrop of the fashion industry, immersing viewers in its vibrant world of haute couture and youthful ambition. In contrast, 'Psycho-Pass' delves into the complexities of a dystopian society governed by an omnipresent surveillance system, exploring themes of justice, morality, and the human psyche. 'Ergo Proxy' transports audiences to a post-apocalyptic world shrouded in mystery and existential uncertainty, where philosophical undertones intertwine with gripping storytelling to unravel the enigma of human identity. Lastly, 'Wonder Egg Priority' offers a surreal and introspective journey into the psyche of adolescence, blending elements of fantasy and psychological drama to illuminate the struggles of youth and the quest for belonging in a fragmented reality.
